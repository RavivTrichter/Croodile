Crocodile Pacman

About

This Pacman game was created by Levav Butbul, Alon Zemer, Afek Surdo And Raviv Trichter. The game is the final project for our course "Advanced Topics In Software Engineering". The uniqueness in our pacman from other pacmans is that you can play in multi-player mode, and additionally to the ghosts you get questions related to our course. Each ghost contains an answer and the player needs to eat the ghost with the correct answer.
